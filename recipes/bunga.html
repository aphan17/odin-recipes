Bun Ga

Description

A Vietnamese dinner staple, a lemongrass chicken noodle soup. Best thing on a cold day or whenever slightly under the weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1 daikon/korean radish
  • 4-5 tbsp of fish sauce
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tbsp of chicken bouillon
  • 1 pack of rice noodles
  • 2 scallions
  • 1 bundle of cilantro/rau ram
  • 3 limes
  • Thai chilis

Steps

  1. Break down chicken into 1-2 inch pieces
  2. Marinate chicken with 4 spoons of fish sauce, shallot and a spoon of black pepper. Set aside for at least 1hr.
  3. Cut up radish into semi moon shapes about 1 inch thick and pound lemongrass to release aroma.
  4. Heat up a pot of water. Add in radish, lemongrass, sugar, and chicken bouillon.
  5. Once boiling, add in chicken. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Season to taste with fish sauce if necessary. Then add toppings of herbs, chili and limes and serve!
